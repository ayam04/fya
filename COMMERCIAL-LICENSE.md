A commercial license removes the noncommercial restriction and grants your
organization the right to use `fya` for the commercial purposes above, for the
agreed term, subject to a signed agreement. Typical terms:

- **Scope:** per-organization use across your teams and internal systems.

- **Support:** as agreed (best-effort by default; priority support optional).
